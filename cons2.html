IT Talent Acquisition


VinfoTech follows different strategies in recruiting methodologies:


Knowledge Based Recruiting Method

Knowledge based recruiting is sharing Information and updating information on an ongoing basis, in order to continuously build a pool of talent. It is targeted search, taken to a new level of client relationship. It is a client-directed, continuous, yet flexible recruiting initiative designed to overcome the problems of critical skill shortage or sustained growth. It delivers a better quality of candidate, value for money, and better time to hire periods.

E-Recruitment / Online Recruitment Method

VinfoTech Talent Team use of technology using tools to assist the recruitment processes. Advertise job vacancies through worldwide web. Candidates send their applications or curriculum vitae through an e-mail.
